Note that \(\psi^{j}_{\alpha}\text{,}\) \(j=1,2\text{,}\) will in general exhibit branch points at points where \(Q_{\alpha}\) vanishes, and so \(\Gamma(\Phi)\) does not in general admit a description in terms of analytic functions near parabolic fixed points of \(\Phi\) in \(\mathbb{T}^2\text{.}\) This is in contrast to the results in [9] which state that level sets of RIFs can be parametrized in such a way at every point of \(\mathbb{T}^2\text{.}\)
