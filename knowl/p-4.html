Our investigation concerns the study of the dynamics certain self-maps of the unit bidisk

\begin{equation*} \mathbb{D}^2=\{(z_1,z_2)\in \mathbb{C}^2\colon |z_j|\lt 1, j=1,2\} \end{equation*}

and seeks to establish some basic facts about their iteration theory. There are many works addressing iteration of analytic self-maps of the bidisk and higher-dimensional polydisks (see, for instance, the papers [1, 4, 12, 14, 15] and the thesis [20] for some background). Our focus is on obtaining detailed results for a restricted class of rational mappings by using elementary means.
