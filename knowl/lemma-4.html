Lemma 4.8.

Suppose \(\lambda \in \mathcal{Z}(Q_{\alpha})\cap (\Lambda^{\sharp}\cap\mathbb{T})\text{.}\) Then \(\phi_{\lambda}(z_1)=e^{-i\alpha}z_1\text{.}\)

Conversely, if \(\phi_{\lambda}(z_1)=e^{-i\alpha}z_1\) for some \(\lambda \in \mathbb{T}\text{,}\) then \(p_2(\lambda)=0\) and \(\lambda \in \mathcal{Z}(Q_{\alpha})\text{.}\)
