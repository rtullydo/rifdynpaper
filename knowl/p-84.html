Consider the polynomials \(p\) and \(\tilde{p}\) in appearing in the RIF \(\phi\) in Example 2.5 in Section 2. There, \(p_1(z_2)=3-z_2\) and \(p_2(z_2)=-1\text{,}\) while \(\tilde{p}_1(z_2)=3z_2-1\) and \(\tilde{p}_2(z_2)=-z_2\text{.}\) Then \(Q_{\alpha}(z_2)=16z_2^2-28z_2+16\text{,}\) and this polynomial has conjugate roots on \(\lambda_{1,2}=\frac{1}{8}(7\pm \sqrt{15}i)\in\mathbb{T}\text{.}\) The corresponding fibers \(F_{\lambda_{1,2}}\) bound the rotation belt of \(\Phi\text{.}\)
