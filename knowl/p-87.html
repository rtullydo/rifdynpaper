Suppose \(\Phi\) is a degree \((1,n)\) RISP with associated \(Q_{\alpha}\) not identically zero. Then the number of rotation belts for \(\Phi\) is less than or equal to

\begin{equation*} \frac{1}{2}\#\left[(\mathcal{Z}(Q_{\alpha}) \setminus \Lambda^{\flat})\cap \mathbb{T}\right]. \end{equation*}
