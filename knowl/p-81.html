Suppose \(Q_{\alpha}(\lambda)=0\) for some \(\lambda \in \mathbb{T}\setminus (\Lambda^{\flat}\cup \Lambda^{\sharp})\text{.}\) Then \(\psi^{1}(\lambda)\) and \(\psi^{2}(\lambda)\) coincide as an element of \(\mathbb{T}\text{,}\) and \(\phi_{\lambda}\) has a parabolic fixed point at \(\psi^1_{\alpha}(\lambda)\text{.}\)
