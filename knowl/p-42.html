The dynamics of a Möbius transformation can be described depending on its type in the above list. Recall that two analytic functions \(f,g\colon \mathbb{D}\to \mathbb{D}\) are conformally conjugate if there exists a conformal map \(\mathfrak{n}\colon \mathbb{D}\to \mathbb{D}\) such that

\begin{equation*} g=\mathfrak{n}\circ f\circ \mathfrak{n}^{-1}. \end{equation*}
